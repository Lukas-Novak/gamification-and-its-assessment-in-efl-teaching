This systematic review examines the effects of gamification in English as a foreign language (EFL) lessons.
It examines the teacher-perceived effects of gamification on pupils' motivation and learning outcomes.
It investigates the satisfaction of EFL teachers with the applicability of gamification platforms. 
11 research articles on this subject matter have been identified and included in the systematic review.
The findings suggest that teachers are satisfied with the applicability of gamification platforms and perceived
positive effect on motivation.
